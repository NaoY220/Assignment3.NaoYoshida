Tropical House Plant

Horsehead philodendron, Moth orchid, Golden pothos...There are many kind of tropical plants which make you feel "Gorgeous" inside your room. Also, you can find the smaller size which you can manage and organize the looks of your room by putting on the table, shelf and even on the wall. For example, Platycerium is a good example that you can put it on the wall using exclusive wall hanging.

Plant Accessories

Do you want to make your room more suited to your needs? Then, find out some accessories which can assist you here. There are diverse of plant pots with different materials and looks. In addition to some accessories on plants, you can find some interior accessories which make you feel some relation to the nature. For example, curtain with leaf patterns and some paints which assist your DIY home-made.

Outdoor Gardening

You are wondering what things you can buy here even if you are the lover gardening, not only house plants? No worry, we have plants and seeds for outside gardening like beautiful flowers, vegetables and even tree. Also, we have some tools which can assist you gardening such watering can, gloves and garden stake which can assist your tree and plant to stand straight. If you are the beginner but have interest on gardening, we are here for supporting you!
